Welcome to the Modal

This is an example of a modal dialog component. It features smooth animations, responsive design, and proper accessibility attributes.

The modal is closed by default and opens when you click the trigger button. You can close it by clicking the X button, pressing the Escape key, or clicking outside the modal.

Modal dialogs are great for confirming actions, displaying forms, or showing important information without navigating away from the current page.
