Modal Dialog Component

Click the button below to open the modal dialog. Use Esc to close, or click outside the dialog.

Example Modal

This is a simple modal dialog. It is closed by default, and opens when you press the button with the "trigger" class.

Keyboard support:

  • Tab and Shift+Tab cycle through focusable elements inside the modal
  • Press Esc to close the modal

Click outside the modal or press the close button to dismiss.
