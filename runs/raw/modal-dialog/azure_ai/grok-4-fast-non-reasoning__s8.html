Example Modal

This is an example modal dialog component. It includes a header with a title and close button, a body with content, and a footer with action buttons.

You can customize the content, styling, and behavior as needed for your application.

The modal is closed by default and opens when the trigger button is clicked. It can be closed by clicking the close button, the overlay, or the cancel button.
