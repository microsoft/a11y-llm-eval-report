Getting Started

▼

Welcome! This widget demonstrates a simple expand/collapse pattern. Click on the header to expand or collapse the content below. You can use this pattern for FAQs, accordions, or any collapsible content on your website.

Features

▼
  • Smooth expand/collapse animations
  • Accessible keyboard navigation
  • Responsive design
  • Custom styling support
  • Easy to integrate

How to Use

▼
  1. Click on any widget header to expand or collapse
  2. The content will smoothly animate in and out
  3. Multiple widgets can be open at the same time
  4. The header changes color when the widget is expanded
