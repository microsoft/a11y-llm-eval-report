Shipping information

We offer free standard shipping on all domestic orders. Expedited options are available at checkout.

  • Standard: 3–5 business days
  • Express: 1–2 business days
  • Overnight: Next business day delivery if ordered before 2 p.m.

Tracking details will be emailed once your package leaves our fulfillment center.

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

To initiate a return:

  1. Log in to your account and select the order.
  2. Choose the items you wish to return.
  3. Download the prepaid shipping label and drop the package at any carrier location.

Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days once the items are received and inspected.
