+ What is your return policy?

Our return policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, we can’t offer you a refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

+ How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, we’ll email you a tracking number. You can use it on the courier’s website to see current progress.

In your account, you can also view tracking details from the Orders page.

+ Can I change my order?

Changes can be made within 24 hours of placing your order. Please contact our support team with your order number and the desired changes.

Note: Some items may not be available for changes once your order is processed.
