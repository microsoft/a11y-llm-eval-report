Accessibility (often abbreviated as A11y) is the practice of making your websites usable by as many people as possible. We often think of this as being about people with disabilities, but the practice of making sites accessible also benefits other groups such as those using mobile devices, or those with slow network connections.

This widget uses the aria-expanded attribute to communicate the state to screen readers. The aria-controls attribute links the button to the content container. JavaScript toggles the hidden attribute and the boolean value of aria-expanded.
