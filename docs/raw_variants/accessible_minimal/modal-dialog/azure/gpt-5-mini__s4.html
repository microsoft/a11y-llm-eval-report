Example Page with Accessible Modal

This example demonstrates an accessible modal dialog. The dialog is closed by default. Use the button below to open it.

Page content

This content will be hidden from assistive technologies while the dialog is open (aria-hidden is toggled) and focus will be trapped inside the dialog until it is closed.

Try navigating with the keyboard: press the button to open the dialog, use Tab and Shift+Tab to move through the controls, press Escape to close, or click outside the dialog.
