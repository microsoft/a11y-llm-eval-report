Example 2: Button-controlled disclosure

This pattern uses a button with aria-expanded and aria-controls to associate it with the content region.

Answers

This content region is hidden when collapsed and is not focusable while hidden.

  • Keyboard: Tab to the button, then press Enter or Space.
  • Focus: You can choose to move focus into the region using the second button.
  • Reflow: The layout stacks cleanly at small widths without horizontal scrolling.

Tip: Press the toggle again to collapse the region.

Manual testing with your keyboard and assistive technology is still recommended.
