Accordion Section: Shipping Information

We offer standard and expedited shipping options to most locations. Please allow 3-5 business days for standard delivery. Expedited shipping (1-2 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee.

International shipping times vary by destination. Duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

For more specific delivery estimates, please use our shipping calculator or contact our customer service team.

Accordion Section: FAQ Item

You may return most new, unopened items sold and fulfilled by us within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items should be in their original packaging.

To start a return, please visit our Returns Center. We'll provide a return shipping label and instructions on how and where to send your package.
