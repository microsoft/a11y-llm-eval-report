Shipping Details

We offer standard shipping (3–5 business days) and express shipping (1–2 business days). Tracking information will be emailed once your order ships.

  • Standard shipping: Free on orders over $50.
  • Express shipping: $12 flat rate.

Return Policy

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

To start a return, contact support and include your order number.
