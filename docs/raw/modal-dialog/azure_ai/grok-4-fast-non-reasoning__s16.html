Example Modal

This is an example of a modal dialog component. It includes a header with a title and close button, a body with content, and a footer with action buttons.

Modals are great for:

  • Confirming actions
  • Displaying forms
  • Showing alerts or notifications
  • Providing additional information

The modal is accessible and includes proper ARIA attributes, keyboard navigation, and focus management.
