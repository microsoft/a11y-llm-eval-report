A Friendly Modal Dialog

Hello there! This is an example of a modal dialog component.

You can close this modal by:

  • Clicking the '×' button in the top right corner.
  • Pressing the 'Escape' key on your keyboard.
  • Clicking anywhere in the shaded area outside this content box.

Modals are useful for displaying important information, collecting user input, or confirming actions without navigating away from the current page.
