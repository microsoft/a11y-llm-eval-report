Weekend Getaway Checklist

Pack smarter and stay organized with this quick checklist before you head out on your weekend adventure:

  • Confirm lodging reservations and check-in times.
  • Pack versatile clothing layers for changing weather.
  • Bring chargers, power bank, and travel adapters.
  • Print or download maps, passes, and itineraries.
