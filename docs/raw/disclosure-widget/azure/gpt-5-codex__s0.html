Expand/Collapse Widget Examples

What is an expand/collapse widget?

An expand/collapse widget allows users to toggle additional information on demand. It's an effective way to keep pages concise while still providing access to detailed content.

  • Click the summary to reveal more.
  • Click it again to hide the details.
  • Useful for FAQs, documentation, and more.
CSS tips for toggle panels

Customize the appearance of your toggle elements to suit your design system. You can restyle the native indicator or replace it with your own icons or text.

Below are a few ideas:

  1. Animate the content to create a smoother reveal.
  2. Change the summary text styling on hover to signal interactivity.
  3. Use thematic icons in the summary::before pseudo-element.
