Berry Blast Smoothie

Blend the following ingredients until creamy and smooth:

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Enjoy immediately for a refreshing start to your day.
