Recipe Overview

Keep the hero section focused while allowing curious cooks to peek at the full ingredient list and method only when they need it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of sea salt
  • Seasonal berries for serving

Method

  1. Combine oats, almond milk, maple syrup, vanilla, and sea salt in a bowl.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for extra creaminess.
  3. Stir well, spoon into bowls, and garnish with fresh berries just before serving.
Tip: Warm the oats in the microwave for 20 seconds if you prefer them slightly heated without cooking them through.
