Expand / Collapse Widget

Examples of an accessible expand/collapse widget. Each widget is wrapped with a div.example and the controlled content container has the class "details".

This is the content controlled by the button above. It is hidden by default and revealed when you activate the control. The implementation keeps aria-expanded and aria-hidden in sync for accessibility.

Tip: Press Space or Enter when focused on the button.

Product dimensions: 120 × 75 × 20 mm. Weight: 210 g. The section expands smoothly and collapses back when toggled. You can include any HTML inside the .details container.

Q: How does this work? A: The script toggles the inline height and aria attributes to animate and remain accessible.
