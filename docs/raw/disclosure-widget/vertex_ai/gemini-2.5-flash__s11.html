Expand/Collapse Widgets

An accordion is a graphical control element comprising a vertically stacked list of items, such as labels or thumbnails. Each item can be "expanded" or "collapsed" to reveal or conceal associated content.

This widget is useful for managing space on a page and presenting a large amount of information in a structured way.

Simply click on the button above each section to toggle its content. The arrow icon will change direction to indicate whether the section is currently expanded or collapsed.

  • Click to show content.
  • Click again to hide content.

Expand/collapse widgets offer several benefits:

  1. Space Saving: They allow you to hide less critical information, decluttering the initial view of a page.
  2. Improved Readability: Users can focus on specific sections of interest without being overwhelmed.
  3. Better User Experience: Provides an interactive way to explore content on demand.

These are especially helpful on mobile devices where screen real estate is limited.
