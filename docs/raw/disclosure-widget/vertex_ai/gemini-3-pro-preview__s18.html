FAQ Widgets

What is the return policy?

You can return any item within 30 days of purchase. The item must be unused and in its original packaging. Please retain your receipt for faster processing.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs will apply, and will be added at checkout. We run discounts and promotions all year, so stay tuned for exclusive deals.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email containing a tracking number. You can use this number on our carrier's website to see the real-time location of your package.
